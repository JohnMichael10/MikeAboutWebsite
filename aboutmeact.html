About Me

Inspiration

Gaming and Anime has had a huge influence on me from a young age. It is particularly from these types of media that kept me hooked that I first started to think of being a com-sci student. Furthermore, it inspired me to be a better person to those around me similar to the actions of the protagonist of these games and anime.

Goal

Thus, it became my goal to rather than play the game, try to make the game itself. To see whether I am capable of making characters that can evoke peoples emotion the same way it did mine. If I can't be the hero then why not make one?

Personal History

A college student that doesn't stop struggling to go on. That's me, John Michael though it might seem cynical, it is what it is. Through the shared efforts of my parents I strived to at least achieve something in life. Thankfully, I'm stubborn and can't give up cause what's the fun of not even trying.
